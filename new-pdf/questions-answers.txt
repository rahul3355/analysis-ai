SECTION 1: DOCUMENT (10 questions — facts from the roadmap)
================================================================================

Q1. What is the target Scope 1 and 2 greenhouse gas emissions reduction for JD Sports UK by 2030, and what is the baseline year?
A1. 50% absolute reduction by 2030, versus the FY2019/20 baseline.

Q2. What is JD Sports UK's SBTi-validated emissions reduction target for 2035 across all scopes?
A2. 67.2% absolute reduction by 2035 across Scope 1, 2, and 3, validated by the Science Based Targets initiative.

Q3. What percentage of JD Sports UK's total carbon footprint does Scope 3 represent, and what is the largest contributor within Scope 3?
A3. Scope 3 represents 98.6% of total emissions. Purchased goods and services (predominantly product sourcing) accounts for 92% of all Scope 3 emissions.

Q4. What is the total budgeted sustainability investment for the UK business over FY2027 to FY2035?
A4. GBP 42 million.

Q5. What is the target for LED lighting retrofit completion across the UK store estate, and what is the estimated annual CO2 saving?
A5. 100% of the UK store estate (430 stores) retrofitted by end of FY2028, saving an estimated 4,200 tCO2e per year.

Q6. What is the target for recycled polyester (rPET) in own-brand products by FY2030 and FY2035?
A6. 50% of own-brand polyester to be recycled (rPET) by FY2030, rising to 75% by FY2035.

Q7. What is the name of JD Sports UK's proposed nationwide product take-back programme and when will it launch?
A7. 'JD Re:Wear', launching in March 2028.

Q8. How many UK colleagues must complete the Level 1 sustainability awareness training by the end of FY2027?
A8. All 8,500 UK colleagues.

Q9. What is the target for packaging weight reduction per dispatched order by FY2030?
A9. 50% reduction by FY2030 (versus FY2025 baseline of 180 grams per order).

Q10. How many 'Sustainability Champions' are to be trained across the UK store network by FY2028, and what is their dedicated time allocation?
A10. 500 Sustainability Champions trained, with 2 hours per week dedicated time for in-store sustainability activities.
